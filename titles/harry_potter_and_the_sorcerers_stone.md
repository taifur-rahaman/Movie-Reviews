"Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" (also known as "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone") follows the orphaned Harry Potter, who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He is whisked away from his ordinary life to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he makes friends, uncovers the truth about his past, and confronts an evil threat tied to a legendary artifact.
